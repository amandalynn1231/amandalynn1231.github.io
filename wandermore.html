THE CHALLENGE

Wandermore began as the product of a 4-hour hackathon, called ideahack, hosted by IdeaLab. Since, I have collaborated with some members of the hackathon team to expand on our original ideas and to bring a design perspective to the project.

THE PROBLEM

How do I become familiar with an area?
How can I experience familiar places in a new way?

THE SOLUTION

To address this problem, we created Wandermore, a mobile application that lets you become as familiar with the neighborhood as the locals. With Wandermore, you can create a tour of a local area with customizations such as a specific end destination, time constraints, and interests.
