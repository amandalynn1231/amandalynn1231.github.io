NUTRISAVINGS MARKETING PORTFOLIO

ui design + dev, ux design

I designed and developed this internal portfolio website for the marketing department at Nutrisavings to archive all of the department's visual work.

SOME FEATURES:

- easy filter nav with dropdowns

- fuzzy search jquery plugins

- simple layout and minimal color use to serve as clean canvas for the portfolio items, colorful and varied in color

- grid with large images to allow the work to standout and be the focus

- one page layout with lightbox details on each item

- worked with marketing team to determine the best nav organization and heirarchy for their needs
